How we work

HARSH TOURS & TRAVELS was established in 2005 as a proprietorship firm
with a registered office in VAPI (Gujarat) in India to expand its car rental
service presence in the growing Indian market by leveraging the
knowhow and expertise that EXCEL TOURS & TRAVELS offers pan India.
HARSH TOURS & TRAVELS operates in India through several cabs according
to customer comfort and needs.

HARSH TOURS & TRAVELS offers a bouquet of services including leasing
solutions, Rent-a-Car, Employee Transportation solutions along with the
capability to offer flexible financing solutions to meet the requirements
the Corporate Lobby.
